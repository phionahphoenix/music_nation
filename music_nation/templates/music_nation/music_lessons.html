 Music Lessons

Music Nation is currently offering CoVid compliant in-home lessons, as well. These lessons are conducted with the teacher wearing a mask, sanitizing frequently, and are conducted outdoors. If you have a patio, backyard, or access to another outdoor space, we can send an instructor to you for learning in person.

We offer music lessons with great teachers, for music instruments/voice for any skill level or age. You can choose from many options, such as piano, guitar, bass, ukulele, violin, viola, cello, wind instruments, drums/percussion, voice, etc.

We also offer coaching in music theory, composition, and arranging for the aspiring writer/composer.
